About Astra — The Firefighter Behind the Brand

At the heart of Astra’s is a woman of action — Ngadiya Kaigama.

Control Room

I began my career in the Control Room, handling emergency calls, dispatching teams, and keeping communication clear and calm.

Business

Astra’s is known for premium quality dried fish and spicy yaji (pepper seasoning), loved by many across Nigeria.

Each product reflects her care and commitment — from packaging to customer satisfaction.

This is where a passion for saving lives meets a love for business and culture.

Operations

I later worked in Operations, facing real fire situations, carrying out rescues, and saving lives.

Public Relations Officer

Today, I serve as a Public Relations Officer (PRO) — engaging the public, promoting safety, and representing our department with pride.

The Astra’s Brand

Astra’s is more than a brand. It’s a story of strength, growth, and balance between service and entrepreneurship.
